Group App -- Boss Battle Trading Card Game

Developed in Unity for Android



Game Description

Boss Battle is a Card Game playable on most Android devices, where you collect humorous monsters and spells of various themes, and use them to battle other players online.

Rules & Regulations


The rules for Boss Battle are still work in progress, but put most simply each player has a consummable amount of mana they can use to cast various monsters and spells. Only monsters in the 'Boss Zone' can use the effects printed on their card, all other monsters can only attack or defend. When the player takes a certain amount of damage, they lose the game!
