Favorite Games

  1. War Thunder
  2. From The Depths
  3. Civilization V

1 -- War Thunder
By far my favorite game for more than a couple reasons. I enjoy the amount skill required to do well at higher tiers, but the ease of entry at lower tiers. It makes the game progress well, and players learn new tactics the more they play the game. The biggest downside is the grind required to unlock higher end vehicles.

2 -- From The Depths
I've played this game as much or possibly more than War Thunder, usually with my brother. It's incredibly difficult, and the learning curve is basically a wall with a mountain at the top, but the satisfaction you get when defeating an enemy with a ship you designed and built is incredible.

3 -- Civilization V
This one is a given, everyone loves Civ V. In my opinion it's one of the best games to play with a group of friends. The DLC are usually good, although they cost money like a lot of other games. My only complaint is that I wish the games would go a little faster, playing a game of Civ takes an entire day sometimes.
